[SECTION: POLICIES]

Refund Policy:
Students are eligible for a full refund within 7 days of enrollment, provided they have not completed more than 10% of the course content. After 7 days, refunds are not guaranteed and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

---

Attendance Policy:
Students are required to attend at least 80% of live sessions to qualify for certification. Attendance is tracked automatically through the learning platform.

---

Certification Policy:
To receive a certificate of completion, students must:
- Complete all required assignments
- Submit and pass the final capstone project
- Meet the minimum attendance requirement

---

Late Submission Policy:
Assignments submitted after the deadline may incur a penalty unless prior approval has been granted by the instructor or support team.

---

Deferral Policy:
Students may request to defer their enrollment to a future cohort if they are unable to continue. Deferral requests must be submitted at least 7 days before the next cohort begins.

---

Withdrawal Policy:
Students who wish to withdraw from a program must notify the support team via email. Withdrawal does not automatically guarantee a refund.

---

Code of Conduct:
All students are expected to maintain respectful and professional behavior. Harassment, plagiarism, or misconduct may result in suspension or removal from the program.

---

Academic Integrity Policy:
Students must submit original work. Any form of cheating or plagiarism will result in disciplinary action, including possible disqualification from certification.

---

Grading Policy:
Student performance is evaluated based on:
- Assignments (40%)
- Participation (20%)
- Capstone project (40%)

A minimum overall score of 60% is required to pass.

---

Access Policy:
Students will retain access to course materials and recordings for up to 6 months after completing the program.

---

Technical Requirements Policy:
Students must have access to a laptop with at least 8GB RAM and a stable internet connection to participate effectively in the program.

---

Support Policy:
Students can reach out for help via email, live chat, or the platform support channel. Response times are typically within 24 hours.

---

Payment Policy:
Students must complete payment before the course start date unless enrolled in an approved installment or financing plan.

---

Scholarship Policy:
Scholarships are awarded based on merit and financial need. Recipients must maintain active participation throughout the program.

---

Job Placement Disclaimer:
While Academy X provides career support and job referrals, job placement is not guaranteed.
