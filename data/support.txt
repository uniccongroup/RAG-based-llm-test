[SECTION: PLATFORM SUPPORT]

Q: I cannot log in to my account. What should I do?
A: Ensure you are using the correct email and password. If the issue persists, try resetting your password using the “Forgot Password” option on the login page.

---

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click on the “Forgot Password” link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.

---

Q: I did not receive the password reset email. What should I do?
A: Check your spam or junk folder. If you still cannot find the email, contact support for assistance.

---

Q: How do I access my learning dashboard?
A: Log in to your account and navigate to the dashboard section where all your enrolled courses and materials are available.

---

Q: I cannot access my course materials. What should I do?
A: Ensure you are properly enrolled in the course. If the issue continues, log out and log back in, or contact support.

---

Q: What should I do if a video is not playing?
A: Check your internet connection, refresh the page, or try accessing the platform using a different browser.

---

Q: The platform is slow or not loading. How can I fix this?
A: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Clear your browser cache or try a different device or browser.

---

Q: How can I contact technical support?
A: You can contact support via email, live chat on the platform, or the support channel available in your dashboard.

---

Q: What is the response time for support requests?
A: Support requests are typically responded to within 24 hours.

---

Q: Can I access the platform on mobile devices?
A: Yes, the platform is accessible on both desktop and mobile devices through a web browser.

---

Q: Can I learn using my phone?
A: Yes, you can access the learning platform using your phone through a web browser. However, for the best experience, especially when working on coding exercises or projects, a laptop or desktop is recommended.

---

Q: What should I do if I encounter a bug on the platform?
A: Report the issue to the support team with details and screenshots if possible. This helps us resolve the issue quickly.

---

Q: How do I update my account information?
A: You can update your profile details in the account settings section after logging in.

---
